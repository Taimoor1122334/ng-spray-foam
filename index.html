Common questions

Questions we hear a lot

Not sure which insulation you need or how it all works? Here are answers to the things people ask us most.

Closed-cell spray foam is highly dense, rigid, acts as an absolute vapor barrier, and delivers a superior R-value of ~7.0 per inch. It's recommended for exterior walls and heavy moisture areas. Open-cell spray foam is lightweight, flexible, serves as an exceptional sound deadener, and provides ~3.8 R-value per inch. It's highly suited for interior walls and roof rafters.

According to building envelope analyses and active client telemetry, retrofitting uninsulated attics and crawlspaces with premium spray foam yields average heating and cooling savings of 35% to 50%. The initial investment pays for itself in lower utility outlays within 3 to 5 years.

Yes, absolutely. Once our premium spray foam cures completely (which takes approximately 24 hours), it becomes 100% inert, odorless, and off-gasses nothing. During application, we ask residents to leave the premises. Once cured, it improves overall respiratory health by blocking dust, pollen, and pests.
